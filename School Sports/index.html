Wawin National School of Excellence

Wawin National School of Excellence is one the prestigious National School of Excellences in the country. Wawin is located in Huon Gulf district Morobe province along the highlands highway. The station 40 mile is where students take a local truck to the school. Wawin National School of Excellence is situated in a peaceful environment where everything is in tune with the nature. The environment makes studying more effective and productive.

Fellowship

Wawin National School of Excellence has various denominations that come together to serve the lord. With a total of 6 denominations namely; TSCF, Catholic, Lutheran, Baptist, Seventhday and Revival student ministries. Wawin provides an environment where students can grow spiritually as well as intellectually. With programs like concerts, crusades and fund-raising nights, students take part in those activities and exposing some of their leadership potential and raise their self esteem and confidence. The TSCF is the ministry that is responsible for organizing every religious activities.

Sports

Wawin National School of Excellence provides the opportunity for students to play various sports games to relieve their schoool pressure. The game varies to make sure every student took part and no student is left out, sports day is every Wednesday 3 - 5pm. Games played includes volleyball, soccer, rugby (only for boys) and basketball. Both genders took part in every games buut only the boys are allowed to play rugby. Other wise these activities provide an environment where students utilize their co-ordinative skills and teamwork to the test...

School Service

Wawin National School of Excellence has school service every Mondays and Thursdays where students take part in beautifying the school campus. There are schoo service where students clean up the schoo campus and dorm service where students work on their respective dorms. These activities teaches students to look after their dorms which subconsiously makes them responsible for their respective area. Thus, teaches students to look after their properties.
